# **以及 (yǐ jí) - as well as; and; and also**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- **以** (yǐ): means "to use," "by means of." Left: **人** (rén, person) radical. Right: "second" (**乙** yǐ).  
- **及** (jí): means "to reach," "and." Left: "to go" radical (**又** yòu, hand from the right). Top-right: "person" radical (**人** rén).

**Examples**:  
- 我(wǒ) 喜欢(xǐ huān) 音乐(yīn yuè) 以及(yǐ jí) 电影(diàn yǐng)。  
- 他(tā) 购买(gòu mǎi)了(le) 书(shū) 以及(yǐ jí) 文具(wén jù)。  
- 我们(wǒ men) 邀请(yāo qǐng)了(le) 老师(lǎo shī) 以及(yǐ jí) 学生(xué shēng)。

**Usage Notes**:  
- **以及** (yǐ jí) joins nouns/noun phrases. More formal than **和** (hé, and).  
- Often used in writing or speeches, not casual speech.

**Memory Aids**:  
- Picture **以** (yǐ, by means of) as a bridge and **及** (jí, reach) as extending a hand, connecting two things "as well as" each other.

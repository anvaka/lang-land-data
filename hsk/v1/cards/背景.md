# **背景 (bèijǐng) - background**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- **背** (bèi): back. Left: 月 (yuè, "flesh" radical); right: 北 (běi, "north", depicting two people back-to-back).  
- **景** (jǐng): scenery, view. Top: 日 (rì, "sun"); bottom: 京 (jīng, "capital"), often associated with a grand view under the sun.

**Examples**:  
- 你的**背景**(bèijǐng)很有趣。(Nǐ de bèijǐng hěn yǒuqù.) - Your background is interesting.  
- 照片的**背景**(bèijǐng)是大海。(Zhàopiàn de bèijǐng shì dàhǎi.) - The background of the photo is the sea.  
- 她有音乐**背景**(bèijǐng)。(Tā yǒu yīnyuè bèijǐng.) - She has a background in music.

**Usage Notes**:  
**背景**(bèijǐng) refers to both literal (visual) and figurative (personal history, context) backgrounds.

**Memory Aids**:  
Imagine someone's “back” (**背**(bèi)) in front of a “scenery” (**景**(jǐng)): their back becomes the background in a view.

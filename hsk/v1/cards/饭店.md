# **饭店 (fàndiàn) - Restaurant / Hotel**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- 饭 (fàn): "food" or "meal". Left side: 饣(shí) radical (food) + right side: 反(fǎn) (upside-down/oppose).  
- 店 (diàn): "shop" or "store". Top: 广(guǎng) (shelter/building) + bottom: 占(zhàn) (occupy).

**Examples**:  
- 这家饭店(fàndiàn)很好吃。(Zhè jiā fàndiàn hěn hǎo chī.) - This restaurant is delicious.  
- 你想去哪个饭店(fàndiàn)吃饭？(Nǐ xiǎng qù nǎge fàndiàn chīfàn?) - Which restaurant do you want to eat at?  
- 饭店(fàndiàn)在火车站(fàndiàn zài huǒchēzhàn)旁边。(Fàndiàn zài huǒchēzhàn pángbiān.) - The restaurant is next to the train station.

**Usage Notes**:  
饭店(fàndiàn) can mean "restaurant" (mostly in Mainland China) or "hotel" (older or formal contexts); context determines which.

**Memory Aids**:  
Imagine 饭(fàn) (meal) + 店(diàn) (shop) = a "meal shop" (restaurant). Picture eating inside a building (广(guǎng)), making it a place for meals.

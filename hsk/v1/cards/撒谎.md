# **撒谎 (sā huǎng) - to lie**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- **撒** (sā): "to scatter; to spread." Left side is "扌" (shǒu, hand radical, left), right side is "散" (sǎn, disperse, right).  
- **谎** (huǎng): "lie; falsehood." Left side is "讠" (yán, speech radical, left), right side is "荒" (huāng, wild, right).

**Examples**:  
- 他( tā) 不( bù) 应该( yīnggāi) 撒谎( sā huǎng)。 (Tā bù yīnggāi sā huǎng.) - He shouldn't lie.  
- 她( tā) 为了( wèile) 朋友( péngyǒu) 撒谎( sā huǎng) 了( le)。 (Tā wèile péngyǒu sā huǎng le.) - She lied for her friend.  
- 孩子们( háizimen) 不( bù) 应该( yīnggāi) 撒谎( sā huǎng)。 (Háizimen bù yīnggāi sā huǎng.) - Children shouldn't lie.

**Usage Notes**:  
- **撒谎** (sā huǎng) is a verb; often used negatively. "说谎" (shuō huǎng) is a synonym, slightly more formal.

**Memory Aids**:  
- **撒** (sā): Imagine "scattering" (撒, sā) the truth.  
- **谎** (huǎng): "Speech" (讠, yán) about "wild" (荒, huāng) things = a lie.

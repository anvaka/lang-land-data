# **星期 (xīngqī) - week**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- **星 (xīng)**: Means "star". Left: 日 (rì, "sun") radical; Right/top: 生 (shēng, "to be born") component.  
- **期 (qī)**: Means "period; phase". Left: 其 (qí, "his/its"), Right/bottom: 月 (yuè, "moon/month").

**Examples**:  
- 今天是星期一。(Jīntiān shì xīngqī yī.) - Today is Monday.  
- 我每星期去游泳。(Wǒ měi xīngqī qù yóuyǒng.) - I swim every week.  
- 下个星期见！(Xià gè xīngqī jiàn!) - See you next week!

**Usage Notes**:  
- 星期 (xīngqī) + number (一 yī, 二 èr, etc.) refers to days of the week (Monday, Tuesday, etc.).  
- Measure word: 个 (gè) as in 下个星期 (xià gè xīngqī, "next week").

**Memory Aids**:  
- Imagine "star (星 xīng) periods (期 qī)" as the stars marking each week in the calendar.  
- "Start your week (星期 xīngqī) by wishing upon a star (星 xīng)!"

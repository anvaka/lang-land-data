# **难过 (nán guò) - Sad, Upset**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- **难 (nán)**: Top = "⺈" (knife radical), bottom = "又" (again/hand) - means "difficult" or "hard".  
- **过 (guò)**: Left = "辶" (walk/movement radical), right = "寸" (inch) - means "to pass" or "to go through".

**Examples**:  
- 他 (tā) 很 (hěn) 难过 (nán guò)。 (Tā hěn nán guò.) - He is very sad.  
- 分手 (fēn shǒu) 让 (ràng) 我 (wǒ) 难过 (nán guò)。 (Fēn shǒu ràng wǒ nán guò.) - Breaking up makes me sad.  
- 看到 (kàn dào) 你 (nǐ) 难过 (nán guò) 我 (wǒ) 也 (yě) 难过 (nán guò)。 (Kàn dào nǐ nán guò wǒ yě nán guò.) - Seeing you upset makes me upset too.

**Usage Notes**:  
- 难过 (nán guò) describes negative emotions (sadness, grief, distress).  
- Common with 很 (hěn) for emphasis: 很难过 (hěn nán guò) = very sad.  
- Not for physical difficulty; use it for emotional difficulty.

**Memory Aids**:  
- "难 (nán)" = difficult + "过 (guò)" = to pass through → difficult to get over = feel sad.

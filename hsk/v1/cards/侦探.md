# **侦探 (zhēn tàn) - Detective**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- **侦** (zhēn): "Investigate." Left: 亻(rén) "person" radical; right: 贞(zhēn) "loyal/faithful."  
- **探** (tàn): "Explore/search." Left: 扌(shǒu) "hand" radical; right: 罙(shēn) "deep" (shows action of searching deeply).

**Examples**:  
- 他(ā)(tā)是(shì)一个(yí gè)侦探(zhēn tàn)。 (Tā shì yí gè zhēn tàn.) - He is a detective.  
- 侦探(zhēn tàn)正在(zhèng zài)调查(diào chá)案件(àn jiàn)。 (Zhēn tàn zhèng zài diào chá àn jiàn.) - The detective is investigating the case.  
- 你(nǐ)喜欢(xǐ huān)侦探(zhēn tàn)小说(xiǎo shuō)吗(ma)？ (Nǐ xǐ huān zhēn tàn xiǎo shuō ma?) - Do you like detective novels?

**Usage Notes**:  
侦探(zhēn tàn) is typically used for professional or fictional detectives (like in novels or movies), not for police officers.

**Memory Aids**:  
Think of 侦(zhēn) as a "person" undertaking true (贞(zhēn)) investigations; 探(tàn) uses "hand" to "search deeply," just like a detective does.

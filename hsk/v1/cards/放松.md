# **放松 (fàngsōng) - relax, to relax**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- **放** (fàng): Means "to release" or "to let go." Left: **攵** (pū, "tap" radical), right: **方** (fāng, "square" or "method").  
- **松** (sōng): Means "loosen," "relax," or "pine tree." Left: **木** (mù, "wood" radical), right: **公** (gōng, "public").

**Examples**:  
- 请 (qǐng) 放松 (fàngsōng)，不要 (búyào) 紧张 (jǐnzhāng)。 (Please relax, don’t be nervous.)  
- 我们 (wǒmen) 周末 (zhōumò) 去 (qù) 放松 (fàngsōng) 一下 (yíxià) 吧 (ba)。 (Let’s go relax this weekend.)  
- 深呼吸 (shēn hūxī) 可以 (kěyǐ) 帮助 (bāngzhù) 你 (nǐ) 放松 (fàngsōng)。 (Deep breathing can help you relax.)

**Usage Notes**:  
**放松** (fàngsōng) is both a verb and an adjective. Often used with verbs like **让** (ràng, "let") or in suggestions.

**Memory Aids**:  
Think **放** (fàng, "release") + **松** (sōng, "loosen") = "release and loosen" → **relax**. Imagine letting go (放, fàng) of tension as a tree (松, sōng) relaxes its branches.

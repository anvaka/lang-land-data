# **密封 (mì fēng) - To seal; sealed**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- **密 (mì):** Top: 宀 (mián, "roof"), Bottom: 必 (bì, "certain"). Together, suggests something tightly covered-hence, "secret" or "close."  
- **封 (fēng):** Left: 寸 (cùn, "inch" or "hand"), Right: 圭 (guī, "jade tablets stacked"). Symbolizes "to enclose with the hand," thus "seal" or "envelope."

**Examples**:  
- 请密封(mì fēng)这个袋子。(qǐng mì fēng zhè ge dàizi.) - Please seal this bag.  
- 信封(mì fēng)要密封(mì fēng)。(xìn fēng yào mì fēng.) - The envelope needs to be sealed.  
- 罐子密封(mì fēng)得很好。(guàn zi mì fēng de hěn hǎo.) - The jar is well sealed.

**Usage Notes**:  
- 密封(mì fēng) is both a verb and an adjective. Often used for packages, containers, or letters to ensure they are airtight or tamper-proof.

**Memory Aids**:  
- 密 (mì): Under the roof (宀 mián), all is secret and shut.  
- 封 (fēng): Enveloped (封 fēng) with a hand (寸 cùn), making a seal.  
- Imagine sealing (密封 mì fēng) a jar tightly so nothing can escape.

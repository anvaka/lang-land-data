# **摇 (yáo) - to shake; to rock; to wave**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- 摇(yáo):
  - Left: 扌(shǒu) "hand" radical, indicating action with the hand
  - Right: 䍃(yáo) phonetic component; top is 爫(zhǎo) "claw," middle is 冖(mì) "cover," and bottom is 木(mù) "wood"

**Examples**:  
- 他(tā) 摇(yáo) 了(le) 摇(yáo) 头(tóu)。 (Tā yáo le yáo tóu.) - He shook his head.  
- 请(qǐng) 摇(yáo) 一下(yíxià) 杯子(bēizi)。 (Qǐng yáo yíxià bēizi.) - Please shake the cup.  
- 树(shù) 被(bèi) 风(fēng) 摇(yáo) 晃(huàng) 起来(qǐlái)。(Shù bèi fēng yáo huàng qǐlái.) - The tree started shaking in the wind.

**Usage Notes**:  
- 摇(yáo) is a verb, often used for moving objects back and forth or up and down.  
- Commonly used in daily actions: 摇头(yáo tóu) (shake head), 摇铃(yáo líng) (ring bell).

**Memory Aids**:  
- Imagine a hand(扌/shǒu) on the left, shaking a tree(木/mù) on the right-together they "摇(yáo)", or shake!

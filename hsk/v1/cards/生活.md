# **生活 (shēnghuó) - life; to live**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- **生** (shēng): life, birth;
  - **Top:** grave (⺈) suggests "come out"
  - **Bottom:** earth (土 tǔ), representing "grow from the ground"  
- **活** (huó): to live, alive;
  - **Left:** water radical (氵), symbolizing "fluid"
  - **Right:** tongue (舌 shé), hinting "activity" or "movement"

**Examples**:  
- 我( wǒ )喜欢( xǐhuān )健康( jiànkāng )的生活( de shēnghuó )。（Wǒ xǐhuān jiànkāng de shēnghuó.）- I like a healthy life.  
- 现在( xiànzài )人们( rénmen )的生活( de shēnghuó )很方便( hěn fāngbiàn )。（Xiànzài rénmen de shēnghuó hěn fāngbiàn.）- People's lives are very convenient nowadays.  
- 学会( xuéhuì )享受( xiǎngshòu )生活( shēnghuó )。（Xuéhuì xiǎngshòu shēnghuó.）- Learn to enjoy life.

**Usage Notes**:  
- **生活** (shēnghuó) is both a noun ("life," "living," "existence") and a verb ("to live").  
- Common in topics about daily routines, quality of life, or lifestyle.

**Memory Aids**:  
- **生** (shēng): Something new (life) "growing from earth."  
- **活** (huó): Life needs "water" (氵) and a "moving tongue" (舌 shé) - like staying active!

# **优秀 (yōu xiù) - Excellent; outstanding**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- **优 (yōu)**: Left side: 亻 (rén páng, "person" radical) indicates relation to people; right side: 尤 (yóu) means "especially" or "particularly." Together, suggests an outstanding person.  
- **秀 (xiù)**: Top: 禾 (hé, "grain/crop" radical), symbolizing growth; bottom: 乃 (nǎi), a phonetic component. As a whole, means "to grow well," thus outstanding or elegant.

**Examples**:  
- 他是一个优秀 (yōu xiù) 的学生 (xué shēng)。 (tā shì yí gè yōu xiù de xué shēng.) - He is an excellent student.  
- 她表现 (biǎo xiàn) 得很优秀 (yōu xiù)。 (tā biǎo xiàn de hěn yōu xiù.) - She performed outstandingly.  
- 优秀 (yōu xiù) 的作品 (zuò pǐn) 令人 (lìng rén) 欣赏 (xīn shǎng)。 (yōu xiù de zuò pǐn lìng rén xīn shǎng.) - Excellent works are admired.

**Usage Notes**:  
优秀 (yōu xiù) is used as an adjective to praise people, qualities, or things that are outstanding or top-class. It often appears before nouns and after 得 (dé) in complement structures.

**Memory Aids**:  
Think of 优 (yōu) as a "person (亻 rén) who stands out (尤 yóu)." 联想 (lián xiǎng): A秀 (xiù) crops (禾 hé) are the best in the field-just like excellent people stand out!

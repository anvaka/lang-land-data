# **回顾 (huí gù) - to look back; to review**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- **回** (huí): "to return", "circle"; Radical 口 (kǒu, mouth) **inside** the outer frame, suggesting returning inside.  
- **顾** (gù): "to look back", "to care"; Radical 页 (yè, page/head) **on the right**, 卩 (jié, seal) **on the left**, originally depicting turning one's head.

**Examples**:  
- 我们经常回顾(huí gù)过去。(Wǒmen jīngcháng huí gù guòqù.) - We often look back at the past.  
- 在总结会上，他回顾(huí gù)了一年的工作。(Zài zǒngjié huìshàng, tā huí gù le yī nián de gōngzuò.) - At the summary meeting, he reviewed a year’s work.  
- 回顾(huí gù)历史有助于未来的发展。(Huí gù lìshǐ yǒu zhù yú wèilái de fāzhǎn.) - Looking back at history helps future development.

**Usage Notes**:  
回顾(huí gù) is usually used formally for retrospection or reviewing events, not simply turning your head. Common in writing, speeches, or summaries.

**Memory Aids**:  
Think: 回(huí, return) + 顾(gù, looking) = "return your gaze" to the past.

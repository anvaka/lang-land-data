# **粒 (lì) - grain; small particle**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- **粒 (lì)**: Left = 米 (mǐ, "rice" radical), symbolizes grain or small particles.
  Right = 立 (lì, "stand"), suggests something standing up or individual.

**Examples**:  
- 一粒米 (yī lì mǐ) - one grain of rice  
- 几粒药 (jǐ lì yào) - several pills (medicines)  
- 每粒种子 (měi lì zhǒngzi) - each seed

**Usage Notes**:  
- **粒 (lì)** is a measure word for small, round objects (grains, pills, seeds).  
- Use after a number or demonstrative, before the noun: 一粒糖 (yī lì táng), 那粒沙 (nà lì shā).

**Memory Aids**:  
- Imagine a *grain* of rice (米 mǐ) *standing up* (立 lì): one tiny particle marked out.

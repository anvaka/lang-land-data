# **每 (měi) - every; each**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- **每** (měi):
  - Top: **⺷** ("mǔ" radical, originally “母”, meaning "mother")
  - Bottom: **毋** ("wú", not or do not)
  - The radical indicates biological or repetitive nature, relating to something recurring.

**Examples**:  
- 我每(měi)天(tiān)跑步(pǎo bù)。(Wǒ měi tiān pǎo bù.) - I run every day.  
- 每(měi)个(gè)人(rén)都(dōu)来了(lái le)。(Měi gè rén dōu lái le.) - Everyone came.  
- 他(tā)每(měi)周(zhōu)学习(xué xí)汉语(hàn yǔ)。(Tā měi zhōu xué xí hàn yǔ.) - He studies Chinese every week.

**Usage Notes**:  
**每** (měi) is usually followed by a measure word (e.g., 个(gè), 天(tiān), 次(cì)) and a noun. It emphasizes repetition or distribution.

**Memory Aids**:  
Think of **每** (měi) as a "mother" (top part) caring for "each" (bottom part) of her children, one by one, every time.

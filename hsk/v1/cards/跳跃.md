# **跳跃 (tiào yuè) - to leap; to jump**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- **跳 (tiào)**: "to jump". Left: 足 (zú, "foot" radical, bottom), indicating movement/action; Right: 兆 (zhào, "omen" or "sign"), provides pronunciation.  
- **跃 (yuè)**: "to leap". Left: 足 (zú, "foot" radical, bottom), showing action; Right: 夭 (yāo, "tender/young", top), suggests pronunciation.

**Examples**:  
- 青蛙 (qīngwā) 跳跃 (tiào yuè) 在水里。 (Qīngwā tiào yuè zài shuǐ lǐ.) - The frog leaps in the water.  
- 他 (tā) 高高地 (gāo gāo de) 跳跃 (tiào yuè) 起来 (qǐ lái)。 (Tā gāo gāo de tiào yuè qǐ lái.) - He leapt up high.  
- 舞者 (wǔ zhě) 在台上 (zài tái shàng) 跳跃 (tiào yuè)。 (Wǔ zhě zài tái shàng tiào yuè.) - The dancer leaps on the stage.

**Usage Notes**:  
"跳跃 (tiào yuè)" describes energetic, upward or forward jumps, often used for animals, sports, or figurative expressions (e.g., ideas leaping forward).

**Memory Aids**:  
Both 跳 (tiào) and 跃 (yuè) share the 足 (zú, "foot") radical, hinting at "foot action"-imagine your feet making big jumps "up" (跃, yuè) and "out" (跳, tiào)!

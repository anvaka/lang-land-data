# **信赖 (xìn lài) - Trust; to trust**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- **信 (xìn)**: "Trust, believe." Left: 亻(rén) "person" radical; right: 言(yán) "speech/words." Suggests "a person speaking words you can trust."  
- **赖 (lài)**: "Rely on, depend on." Left: 亻(rén) "person" radical; right: 赖(lài) combines 来(lái) "come" and 贝(bèi) "shell/money," hinting at relying for goods.

**Examples**:  
- 我(wǒ) 信赖(xìn lài) 你(nǐ)。 (wǒ xìn lài nǐ) - I trust you.  
- 他(tā) 是(shì) 值得(zhí dé) 信赖(xìn lài) 的(de) 朋友(péng yǒu)。 (tā shì zhí dé xìn lài de péng yǒu) - He is a trustworthy friend.  
- 我们(wǒ men) 互相(hù xiāng) 信赖(xìn lài)。 (wǒ men hù xiāng xìn lài) - We trust each other.

**Usage Notes**:  
信赖(xìn lài) is usually used for people, reflecting deep trust. Often formal, sometimes interchangeable with 信任(xìn rèn).

**Memory Aids**:  
信(xìn)=person+speech: "A person's promise you trust."  
赖(lài): Think "rely" on someone to "come (来(lái)) with shells (贝(bèi))" (valuables).

# **急于求成 (jí yú qiú chéng) - Eager for quick success**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- **急** (jí): Urgent; top: "氵"(water), bottom right: "刍" (grass), signifying "pressing" like water rushing over grass.  
- **于** (yú): At, in, on; simple character, central vertical stroke with a small horizontal on top, common preposition.  
- **求** (qiú): Seek; left: "氺"(water), right: a hand grabbing, meaning "to search/strive."  
- **成** (chéng): Succeed/complete; left: "戈"(spear), right: "丁", meaning accomplishment through action.

**Examples**:  
- 他(tā)太(tài)急于(jí yú)求成(qiú chéng)了(le)，结果(jié guǒ)反而(fǎn'ér)失败(shī bài)了(le)。 (tā tài jí yú qiú chéng le, jié guǒ fǎn’ér shī bài le.) - He was too eager for quick success and ended up failing.  
- 做事(zuò shì)不能(bù néng)急于(jí yú)求成(qiú chéng)。 (zuò shì bù néng jí yú qiú chéng.) - You shouldn't rush things for quick results.  
- 急于(jí yú)求成(qiú chéng)只会(zhǐ huì)带来(dài lái)压力(yā lì)。 (jí yú qiú chéng zhǐ huì dài lái yā lì.) - Being eager for quick success only brings stress.

**Usage Notes**:  
Often used as criticism-don’t push for immediate results (negative connotation); usually modifies people's actions or attitudes.

**Memory Aids**:  
Imagine (急 jí) someone urgently (于 yú: on the way to) chasing (求 qiú) a trophy (成 chéng: accomplishment), tripping because they rushed-don’t chase success too quickly!

# **免费 (miǎn​fèi) - Free (of charge)**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- **免 (miǎn)**: Top: ⺈; Bottom: 儿 (ér, “child” radical). Means "to exempt" or "to avoid."  
- **费 (fèi)**: Left: 贝 (bèi, “shell/money” radical); Right: 弗 (fú). Means "fee" or "expense."

**Examples**:  
- 这本书是免费 (miǎn​fèi) 的。(Zhè běn shū shì miǎnfèi de.) - This book is free.  
- 你可以免费 (miǎn​fèi) 试用七天。(Nǐ kěyǐ miǎnfèi shìyòng qī tiān.) - You can try it for free for seven days.  
- 免费 (miǎn​fèi) 饮料在那边。(Miǎnfèi yǐnliào zài nàbiān.) - Free drinks are over there.

**Usage Notes**:  
- 免费 (miǎn​fèi) is an adjective and often used with nouns (e.g., 免费 午餐 (miǎn​fèi wǔcān) - free lunch).  
- Common in ads, promotions, and public services.

**Memory Aids**:  
- 免 (miǎn) looks like a child (儿, ér) ducking to avoid a fee (费, fèi), making it “free.”

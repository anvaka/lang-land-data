# **以前 (yǐ qián) - before; formerly; previously**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- **以** (yǐ): "to use; according to." Left: 亻(rén, person radical); Right: 𠂉(yǐ, to extend).  
- **前** (qián): "front; before." Top: ⺧(niú, cow); Middle: 月(yuè, moon/flesh); Bottom: 刀(dāo, knife).

**Examples**:  
- 我(wǒ) 以前(yǐ qián) 住(zhù) 在(zài) 北京(Běi jīng)。  
- 以前(yǐ qián) 我(wǒ) 不(bù) 会(huì) 游泳(yóu yǒng)。  
- 你(nǐ) 以前(yǐ qián) 见(jiàn) 过(guò) 他(tā) 吗(ma)?

**Usage Notes**:  
以前(yǐ qián) is used before verbs or at the beginning of sentences to indicate a past time. It’s equivalent to "before" or "in the past."

**Memory Aids**:  
以前(yǐ qián): Imagine using 以(yǐ, to use) the 前(qián, front) part of your memories to talk about before. Imagine "using the front" as accessing things that came before now.

# **里 (lǐ) - inside; in**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- 里 (lǐ): Top is "田 (tián)" (field), bottom is "土 (tǔ)" (earth/soil). The character originally depicts a village inside a field, indicating “inside, within.”

**Examples**:  
- 他在家里。(Tā zài jiā lǐ.) - He is at home.  
- 书包里有书。(Shū bāo lǐ yǒu shū.) - There are books in the backpack.  
- 我们学校里很大。(Wǒ men xué xiào lǐ hěn dà.) - Our school is very big (inside).

**Usage Notes**:  
里 (lǐ) usually follows a noun to indicate location inside something. Common in structures: [Place] + 里 (lǐ).

**Memory Aids**:  
Imagine a field (田 tián) and soil (土 tǔ) together-things are “inside” the earth or “in” the field, like being “inside” a place.

# **领会 (lǐng huì) - Comprehend; Grasp; Understand**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- 领 (lǐng): "neck; to lead"
  - Left: 纟(sī) silk radical
  - Right: 令 (lìng) "order" phonetic  
- 会 (huì): "meet; can; understand"
  - Top: 人 (rén) person radical
  - Bottom: 云 (yún) cloud

**Examples**:  
- 我 (wǒ) 领会 (lǐng huì) 了 (le) 你的 (nǐ de) 意思 (yì si)。 (wǒ lǐng huì le nǐ de yì si.) - I understood your meaning.  
- 他 (tā) 很快 (hěn kuài) 领会 (lǐng huì) 了 (le) 技巧 (jì qiǎo)。(tā hěn kuài lǐng huì le jì qiǎo.) - He quickly grasped the skill.  
- 领导 (lǐng dǎo) 要求 (yāo qiú) 我们 (wǒ men) 领会 (lǐng huì) 精神 (jīng shén)。(lǐng dǎo yāo qiú wǒ men lǐng huì jīng shén.) - The leader asks us to comprehend the spirit.

**Usage Notes**:  
- 领会 (lǐng huì) emphasizes deep understanding, often of abstract ideas or intentions-not just surface comprehension.

**Memory Aids**:  
Think of 领 (lǐng) "leading" your mind to 会 (huì) "meeting" an idea: to truly 领会 (lǐng huì) is to lead yourself into a meeting with understanding.

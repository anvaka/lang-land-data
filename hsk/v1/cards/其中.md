# **其中 (qí zhōng) - among them; in it**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- **其 (qí)**: "its"/"that"; top: 亠 (tóu, lid), middle: 八 (bā, eight) and 土 (tǔ, earth) stacked vertically under the lid; bottom: 儿 (ér, child/legs).  
- **中 (zhōng)**: "middle"/"center"; outer: 口 (kǒu, enclosure) serves as a box; through the center vertically is a line representing “middle”.

**Examples**:  
- 班上有二十个学生，其中(qí zhōng)五个是女孩。(Bān shàng yǒu èrshí gè xuéshēng, qí zhōng wǔ gè shì nǚhái.) - There are 20 students in the class, among them five are girls.  
- 我喜欢很多运动，其中(qí zhōng)最喜欢篮球。(Wǒ xǐhuān hěn duō yùndòng, qí zhōng zuì xǐhuān lánqiú.) - I like many sports, among them I like basketball the most.  
- 这些问题，其中(qí zhōng)有一个很难。(Zhèxiē wèntí, qí zhōng yǒu yī gè hěn nán.) - Of these questions, one is very difficult.

**Usage Notes**:  
- 其中(qí zhōng) is used to refer to one or more items/parts from within a previously mentioned group.  
- Often placed before mentioning a specific element(s).

**Memory Aids**:  
Think of 其(qí) as “its possession,” and 中(zhōng) as “in the middle”; so 其中(qí zhōng) = “in the midst of it/them.”

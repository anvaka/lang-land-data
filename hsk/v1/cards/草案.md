# **草案 (cǎo àn) - draft (document/proposal)**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- 草 (cǎo): grass; top: 艹 (cǎo, "grass" radical); bottom: 早 (zǎo, "early"-here for sound).  
- 案 (àn): plan, table; top: 木 (mù, "wood" radical); bottom: 安 (ān, "peace").

**Examples**:  
- 我们已经准备好草案。(Wǒmen yǐjīng zhǔnbèi hǎo cǎo'àn.) - We have already prepared the draft.  
- 法律草案正在讨论中。(Fǎlǜ cǎo'àn zhèngzài tǎolùn zhōng.) - The draft law is under discussion.  
- 他们提出了一个新的草案。(Tāmen tíchūle yí gè xīn de cǎo'àn.) - They proposed a new draft.

**Usage Notes**:  
草案 (cǎo'àn) is formal, used for official or legal drafts. Attach 草案 (cǎo'àn) after the document type, e.g., 方案草案 (fāng'àn cǎo'àn, "project draft").

**Memory Aids**:  
Imagine 草 (cǎo, "grass") as rough, unfinished; combine with 案 (àn, "table/plan") to mean a "rough plan" = draft.

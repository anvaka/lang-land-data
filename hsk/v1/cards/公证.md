# **公证 (gōngzhèng) - Notarization; Notary**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- **公 (gōng)**: "public; fair." Top: 八 (bā, divide), bottom: 厶 (sī, private). Together: fairness, impartiality.  
- **证 (zhèng)**: "evidence; prove." Left: 讠 (yán, speech, radical), right: 正 (zhèng, correct). Speech + correct = giving correct evidence.

**Examples**:  
- 我们需要公证这份合同。(Wǒmen xūyào gōngzhèng zhè fèn hétóng.) - We need to notarize this contract.  
- 他带来了公证文件。(Tā dàilái le gōngzhèng wénjiàn.) - He brought a notarized document.  
- 公证处明天休息。(Gōngzhèngchù míngtiān xiūxi.) - The notary office is closed tomorrow.

**Usage Notes**:  
- **公证 (gōngzhèng)** is a noun or verb: "to notarize," "notarization." Used in legal, official contexts.  
- **公证处 (gōngzhèngchù)** = notary office.

**Memory Aids**:  
- Think: 公 (gōng, public) + 证 (zhèng, proof) = official proof for public/legal matters.  
- Imagine a judge making a 公 (gōng, public) announcement of 证 (zhèng, evidence).

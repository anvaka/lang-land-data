# **模糊 (mó hú) - blurry; vague; unclear**

**Character Breakdown**:  
- **模** (mó): "model; pattern." Left: "木" (mù, wood) radical, right: "莫" (mò, 'not' / a phonetic).  
- **糊** (hú): "paste; muddled." Left: "米" (mǐ, rice) radical, right: "胡" (hú, a surname or 'reckless' / phonetic).

**Examples**:  
- 图像很模糊。 (Túxiàng hěn mó hú.) - The image is blurry.  
- 他说得很模糊。 (Tā shuō de hěn mó hú.) - He spoke vaguely.  
- 我对那个记忆有些模糊。 (Wǒ duì nà ge jìyì yǒu xiē mó hú.) - My memory of that is a bit unclear.

**Usage Notes**:  
- **模糊** (mó hú) is most often an adjective, meaning "blurry" (visual), "vague/unclear" (ideas, speech), or "fuzzy" (memory).

**Memory Aids**:  
- Picture a "model" (**模** (mó)) made from "rice paste" (**糊** (hú)): if wet, details become blurry-just like being **模糊** (mó hú).
